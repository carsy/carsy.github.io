about me

Hi! My name is José Bateira, I'm 23 years old and I'm from Valongo, Porto, Portugal. I'm currently working on my thesis "Spotify-ed: Music Recommendation and Discovery in Spotify" as part of my IT Engineering course at FEUP.

My mind wasn't set on computer engineering from the beginning: I was also interested in Music which is still very important to me and present in my life.

I finished my first year of the course at University of Minho in 2010, and then started my second year of the course at FEUP.

My main areas of interest are Web Development, Web Design, Linux and Mobile Development.

I'm a member at the IEEE Student Branch group of the University of Porto - NuIEEE. It's a lot of fun to work on extracurricular projects/activities with friends that share the same enthusiasm over technology and software development.
